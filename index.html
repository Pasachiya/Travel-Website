More About Us

Shashi Lanka Tours is a newly established company dedicated to providing unparalleled tour experiences in Sri Lanka. With our exceptional tour packages, you can explore the breathtaking beauty of our country. Thank you for entrusting us with your travel plans.

Transport

Foods

Our packages are meticulously designed to maximize your time and benefits. We organize travel itineraries throughout Sri Lanka, ensuring seamless exploration. Additionally, we handle entrance tickets to various sites, ensuring a hassle-free experience for our clients.

We prioritize your safety throughout our travels, implementing strict safety measures and providing vigilant assistance to ensure a secure and enjoyable experience for all.
